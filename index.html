Learning through play is fundamental to our children's education, helping them to develop the necessary skills in life. Puppets can stimulate children's imagination, encourage creative play and discovery and are a wonderful interactive way to introduce narrative to even the most reluctant reader. They can be a powerful way of bringing story time to life; puppets can provide a focus for role play, encouraging the child's imagination and involvement in activities and can play a fundamental part in story telling, an escencial skill for life.

Finger puppets bring two additional benefits, parents and caregivers can adquiere a wide variety of puppets for a low cost. This allow for a wider variety of puppet characters for children to play and learn with.

Our finger puppets are made out of all natural and fully renewable materials. They are carefully crafted by expert artisans. Adding a dimenstion sustanability and greeness to this fun activity.

FAQ

What are you finger puppets made of?
Our finger puppets are made of alpaca and llama wool.
Can I custom order a puppet character?
Yes, if you send us a picture of the character you would like along with your request, we will create that new finger puppet character for you.
